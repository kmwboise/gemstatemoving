About Us
"For all your moving needs"

Gem State Moving is locally owned and operated. For 20 years Gem State has been service the entire state of Idaho and eleven surrounding western states.

Leroy Jansen, who has over 34 years of experience, owns Gem State Moving. He has been a member of the BBB Accredited business for 16 years with an A+ rating.

Gem State Moving takes pride in what they do and will always work to make sure their customers are satisfied.

Leroy Jansen - Owner

Leroy Jansen is born and raised in Idaho and has been in the moving business since 1979. Leroy worked for Allied Van Lines for 12 years before starting his own business in 1993. He brings experience and knowledge to all of his customers.

Whether your moving your residence or business, Leroy can help take some stress off your shoulders with is passion for the business.
